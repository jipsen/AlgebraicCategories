=====Sets=====

Abbreviation: **Set**
====Definition====
A \emph{set} is a structure $\mathbf{A}=\langle A\rangle$ with no operations or relations defined on $A$.

==Morphisms==
Let $\mathbf{A}$ and $\mathbf{B}$ be sets. A morphism from $\mathbf{A}$ to $\mathbf{B}$ is a function $h:A\rightarrow B$.
